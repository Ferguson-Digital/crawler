APPLE
 * iPhone 6s and above (excluding iPhone SE)
 * iOS version 12 and above
 * Apple Watch series 3 and above
 * WatchOS 5.1 and above

ANDROID
 * Android OS 9 and above
 * Minimum diagonal screen size: 5.7 (inches)
 * Minimum aspect ratio: 18:9
 * Minimum screen resolution: 2880 x 1440 (pixels)
